Chapathi and soyachunks

protein content: 7.4g/100g and 52g/100g

Soya chunks curry is a flavor-filled dish that is made with soya chunks or meal maker cooked in a delicious indian sauce. You can enjoy this curry with roti or rice for an easy healthy dinner!

prep time

5 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

1 servings

instructions

step 1

Add two cups of wheat flour to a bowl, add a pinch of salt and mix everything.Add water little by little and mix slowly: Don't worry if the dough sticks to your hands. Knead the dough for at least 5 minutes.leave it for 20 minutes and roll the dough using rolling pin.

step 2

boil the soyachunks and let it cool and drain the hot water and replace it with normal water and let it cool again.Finally drain the water in soyachunks with hands and now it is ready to eat

step 3

Enjoy your chapathi and soyachunks with ketchup.

ingredients

wheat

soyachunks

ketchup

tools

A stove

chapathi making utensils
