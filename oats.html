oat meal

protein content: 2.4g/100g

Oatmeal is a very popular breakfast food that consists of oats and a liquid, such as water, cow's milk, or plant-based milk. Full of nutrients and fiber, oats are one of the most nutritious whole-grain foods that a person can consume.

prep time

1 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

1 servings

instructions

step 1

Bring water or milk to a boil in a medium saucepan.

step 2

Stir in oats, reduce heat to low. Simmer uncovered over low heat, stirring occasionally, for 10-15 minutes or until oats are of desired texture.

step 3

Enjoy your oatmeal with delicious toppings.
